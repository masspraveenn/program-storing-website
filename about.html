TiHAN Autonomous Campus Shuttle

  • The TiHAN Autonomous Campus Shuttle at IIT Hyderabad is a groundbreaking initiative that showcases the institute's commitment to advancing autonomous transportation technology.
  • This shuttle, part of the Technology Innovation Hub for Autonomous Navigation Systems (TiHAN), represents a significant step forward in integrating autonomous vehicles into everyday campus life.
  • Designed to enhance mobility within the campus, the shuttle combines sophisticated navigation technologies with a focus on sustainability.
  • It operates with minimal human intervention, relying on advanced sensors and real-time data processing to navigate safely and efficiently.
  • As a testament to IIT Hyderabad's innovation, the TiHAN Autonomous Campus Shuttle not only provides a glimpse into the future of autonomous transport.
